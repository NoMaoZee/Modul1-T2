Getting to Know Indonesia's Tree Diversity: A Sustainable Natural Heritage

Indonesia has an incredible biodiversity, including various types of trees that are unique and distinctive. Each tree has its own story and benefits for human life and the environment. Let's explore the richness of Indonesia's nature through interesting information about the trees that are the are icons of the country's beauty and sustainability.

01

Pohon Meranti (Shorea spp.)

Meranti is one of the most important commercial trees found in the tropical rainforests of Indonesia. found in Indonesia's tropical rainforests. Its wood is often used in the timber industry, including furniture and construction.

02

Pohon Ulin (Eusideroxylon zwageri)

Also known as ironwood, the ironwood tree is well known for its its strength and resistance to water. Often used for building materials for traditional Borneo houses.

03

Pohon Jati (Tectona grandis)

Teak trees are known for their strong and durable wood. Widely used in the manufacture of luxury furniture and boats.

04

Pohon Damar (Agathis dammara)

This tree produces a resin that is often used in the paint industry, varnish, and handicraft industries. Widely found in Sulawesi and Maluku.

05

Pohon Cendana (Santalum album)

Sandalwood is prized for its distinctive aroma and is used in perfumes as well as religious ceremonies.
